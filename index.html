OUR MISSION: AUTONOMOUS DERAILMENT PREVENTION

Traditional railway telemetry focuses purely on logging and reports. Sensor data is streamed to a distant server, plotted on a chart, and left for human operators to interpret. By the time an operator flags an anomaly, it is too late.

When extreme summer heat causes rail tracks to expand and buckle, or when continuous high-speed traffic causes joint fractures, a static report cannot stop an approaching express train running at 110 km/h with thousands of passengers. Every second of human confirmation delay adds hundreds of meters to the train's braking trajectory.

RailGuard AI changes the paradigm. We build the cognitive infrastructure. Solar-powered edge-sensing nodes classify physical track stress locally using on-chip TinyML neural networks, while our multi-agent AI loop processes live railway network data, evaluates risk, overrides sector signals to RED, and applies emergency braking in under 5 seconds.

THE COGNITIVE PIPELINE

STAGE 01 — EDGE SENSING

High-Frequency Vibration & Strain Capture

Custom telemetry nodes, clamped magnetically to the web of the rail, continuously sample 3-axis acceleration and gyroscope metrics at 100Hz. Ambient conditions are tracked side-by-side to correlate environmental stressors like track temperature and flood levels.

  • 3-axis raw IMU sampling every 10ms
  • Magnetic clamp — zero-tool installation on live track
  • IP68 rugged aluminum enclosure
  • Ultra-low-power sleep with wake-on-vibration trigger
VIB 1.02G TEMP 34°C 100Hz
STAGE 02 — EDGE TinyML

On-Chip Real-Time Classification

The ESP32 runs a quantized neural network locally — evaluating physical vibrations and detecting fracture, buckle, and displacement classes in under 9ms without cloud dependency.

  • 8-bit quantized CNN runs entirely on-chip
  • FFT-based spectral analysis done at the edge
  • 98.1% accuracy classifying joint fractures and buckling
  • Operates independently of cellular networks

THE HARDWARE INTERFACE

Stark, industrial hardware components designed from scratch for extreme railway environments in India.

NODE

RailGuard Edge Node v1.2

Rugged IP68 aluminum enclosure, magnetically clamped to the rail web with 50kg neodymium anchors. Zero-tool installation, 90-day battery life without solar.

  • ESP32-S3 Dual-Core 240MHz
  • MPU-6050 6-DOF IMU at 100Hz
  • 18650 LiFePO4 + 2W solar panel
  • Operating range: -20°C to 70°C
COMMS

LoRaWAN Mesh Gateway

Solar-powered repeater nodes spaced every 1.5km to form a self-healing mesh network — ensuring alert delivery even during complete cellular blackout zones on remote SCR sections.

  • 868 / 915 MHz LoRa long range
  • 12km point-to-point range
  • Cryptographically signed packets
  • Self-healing dynamic mesh routing
OVERRIDE

Locomotive Cab Receiver

Installed in the locomotive cabin, wired directly into the brake circuit. Monitors the regional RailGuard RF channel and triggers vacuum brakes automatically on receiving a signed emergency packet.

  • Redundant dual RF receiver
  • Direct vacuum brake relay trigger
  • LED + audio alert in cab dashboard
  • Tamper-proof encrypted command auth
